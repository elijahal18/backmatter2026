title: A Veil Between Worlds
author: Katya Wack

My mind wanders,
Where you’re going;
I can’t see you in my world.

Trepid skies form above,
And wind blusters against the window panes.
I nestle deeper in my duvet.

Patiently, I listen for your voice,
Messages disguised in the willows. 
I translate:

You say you’re waiting in the mountains;
If I search I will find you,
Amongst the birch trees in the wood.

You’re the waves lapping at the coastline;
If I dive in you will hold me,
A steady current keeping me afloat.

You’re the bee buzzing in my ear,
If I listen I will hear you,
A secret language between us two.

The storm lumbers on outside my window,
So I search between the raindrops;
Squinting past the naked eye.

Out of the darkness comes a rainbow,
A vibrant signal cloaked in sunshine;
A helping hand to guide me home.

Now I see clearly, you surround me.
Travelling through kaleidoscopic dreamscapes,
In a veiled dance between our worlds.
